Restaurant Checklist

About

This is a tool to keep track of your visited restaurants, search through a list of restaurants and pick the ones you would like to visit. Cross off any of the restaurants you have already visited and rate them to create your personal favorite restaurants list.
Easily find restaurants in your list which you still would like to visit. Compare the list with your friends and see where you can enjoy your next meal.

Search Restaurants

Start by searching through a list of restaurants and pick your favorite restaurants to select the ones that you would like to visit!

Manage Your List

Rate the restaurants which you have visited. And see which ones you like the most.

Plan Your Next Trip

Plan which restaurant you would like to visit next!
